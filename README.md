# Brittleness of Ionic Compounds

This repository features a research assignment exploring the **mechanical behavior of crystal lattice structures**. It examines the fundamental chemistry of ionic bonding, the specific mechanisms that lead to material fracture under stress, and the industrial trade-offs between high electrical resistance and limited durability.

---

###  Key Highlights

* **Bonding Mechanics**: Analyzes how electron transfer and electrostatic forces create rigid, brittle structures.
* **Industrial Applications**: Discusses the use of ionic compounds in **electronic insulators** and **abrasive cutting tools**.
* **Engineering Challenges**: Identifies limitations such as **thermal shock** and lack of flexibility in aerospace or medical applications.
* **Future Solutions**: Reviews modern techniques like **doping** and **composite material design** to enhance ductility.
